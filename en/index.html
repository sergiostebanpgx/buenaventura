SUSTAINABILITY

We apply industry best practices to ensure the sustainability of our operations.

We continued to work hard to achieve a "Culture of Safety" that involves all members of Buenaventura family.

We have mantained cordial and trust-based relationships with communities located near our operations; and promoting sustainable development as part of our emphasis on shared social responsibility.

We continued to apply best industry practices, seeking to innovate in our water management and mine closure plans to guarantee the sustainability of our operations.
